This pages tells the story about the Singapore Population composition. The page can answer a few questions: What is the male to female popualation ratio looks like? What is the total trend of population growth in Singapore? What are different ethnicity lives in Singapore and who are the majority of them? What is the Gender, Age and Ethnicity composition looks like? There are going to be three pages:

Males vs Female Population Trend: This page gives information about how the male vs female population growth looks like from 1970 till 2015.

Ethnicity, Age Group Population Trend: This page gives information about in each Age group how the ethnicity looks like.

Ethnicity Growth Over Years: This page gives information about different Ethnicity population grow over time from 1970 till 2015.


Data values are taken from public data available from Singapore government website https://data.gov.sg/dataset/

Visualization Pointers

Narrative structure – The narration follows an interactive slide show. There are 4 different slides that are present in the narration. Each slide can be viewed by clicking the names of the slides on the navigation bar. Male vs Female Growth, Ethnicity Growth has some free form exploration like tool tips on the line charts. Ethnicity vs Age Group has the year and toggle switch to see different years and how the age group vs ethnicity looks like. You can toggle to sort the data which tells age group that has the highest population in that year.
Parameters - Different parameters are present in the narrative visualization. Hovering over the different scene names also makes the links turn into gray option which enables the user to understand that they can be clicked. All the slides show the charts with a transition and delay so that immediately when the user has arrived at the scene, the chart kind of blinks up to catch user’s attention. In the Age vs Ethnicity page, the year can be changed and that provides an ability to change the state of the page to show only certain details to the user.
Triggers – Couple of triggers are available in each of the scenes. In the Sex Ratio and Ethnicity Growth pages: Hovering the mouse over the line charts, triggers tooltips to show details like the population actual counts between the different groups displayed in the chart. In the Age vs Ethnicity page, there are couple of options available. Like you can choose between different years in the drop-down list box to see how the age group flared between years for each of the ethnicity group. Also, there is a toggle check box, that can be used to sort the order of the bars so that the highest is displayed to understand how the growth rate is impacted each year
Scenes – The scenes are ordered from the overall view to a much-detailed view in the end. First page which is called as the home page showcases what are the three different scenes that present. And also provides some information about the inferences that has been arrived. The first scene gives an overall population count between a generic metric like male vs female population. 2nd and 3rd scene of the narration is more of a detailed depth about the different ethnicity groups that are present in Singapore and two different data points for the same is shown in relation to the population count. 2nd scene only shows out of these male vs female population, what are the different ethnicity groups are present in Singapore. 3rd scene shows the different age group within that ethnicity as well as across years how the transition has happened in all the 3 scenes. All the scenes use year as a base dimension. Everything is shown as a trend over the years and how the growth looks over years.
Annotations – Annotations are provided for each scene depending on what information is shown in the page. Home page has provided an overall information about the pages that are about to follow. Male vs Female Growth and Ethnicity Growth pages uses tooltips to show the actual population count over years from 1957 to 2018. Annotation change within a scene. Once they are shown when the mouse is hovered over and the annotations are removed when the mouse is out of the SVG. Ethnicity vs Age Group page shows the total population value on top of the bars for each age group. It changes for every year when its selected from the list drop down option.
